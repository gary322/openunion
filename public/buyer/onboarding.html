Checklist

~10 minutes
  1. Create your platform org + first API key: POST /api/org/register
  2. Verify your domain (required for publishing bounties): POST /api/origins then POST /api/origins/:id/check
  3. (Optional) Allow third‑party browser UIs for your org: PUT /api/org/cors-allow-origins
  4. Set your platform fee (your cut) + wallet: PUT /api/org/platform-fee
  5. Register your app type (no admin approval needed): POST /api/org/apps
  6. Create and publish bounties/jobs: POST /api/bounties, POST /api/bounties/:id/publish
  7. Monitor activity: Admin Apps dashboard and Apps list
  8. Disputes posture: default 1‑day hold window (production). During hold you can open a dispute; after hold, refunds auto‑run as refund = gross - Proofwork fee. Buyer tools: Buyer portal → Disputes.
